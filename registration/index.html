C2SHIP Meeting 2023
October 26-27, 2023
C2SHiP Annual Meeting, Washington DC
Home Registration Program Venue Contact

Registrations to the event are free.
Deadline for abstract submission is midnight (Pacific time) on September 10th, 2023.
If your abstract is selected, you are invited to present your poster in person in Washington DC.
Please note that authors whose abstracts are accepted for presentation will be expected to attend the meeting in person present their poster.
Even if your abstract is not accepted, you will be invited to register for the event and attend in-person or online to use the opportunity to connect with students, trainees, researchers and industrial partners from the C2Ship organization.
For more information about C2SHIP please visit C2SHIP.org
